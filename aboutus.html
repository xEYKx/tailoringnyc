About Us



We started out working with Heights + Kenchi, a bespoke & custom suiting and shirting company based in New York City in 2009 doing any and all of the alterations and touch ups for their clients custom clothing.

After several years of closely working together and watching their suiting business grow, we branched off and started TAILORING NYC. We cater to a wide range of clients doing all types of alterations. From re-cutting old suits to slim them down and give them a more modern fit to hand stitching small tears in a knitted sweater, we can do it all. We have a stellar reputation online. Although the reviews are under Heights + Kenchi, the work is by us.

No job is too big, no job is too small.  We do all types of tailoring & alterations. From vintage pieces to high-end & luxury brands. All alterations are done by an alteration specialist who prioritizes fit, craftsmanship and your personal satisfaction.  Sign up today and let our tailors do the alteration work for you.

Our philosophy & commitment to you is simple: Ensure a perfect fit by way of alteration & tailoring. We take our time with each garment to make sure that you are satisfied with the end result.  You trust us enough to give us your business, let us provide you with world class tailoring & alterations and a positive experience.
